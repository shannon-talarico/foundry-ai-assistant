# Production Partners

## Overview

Production partners play a critical role in helping creators bring products to market.

While creators focus on building audiences, products, and brands, production partners provide the operational capabilities required to manufacture, customize, fulfill, and deliver products.

Examples include:

* Print shops
* Embroidery businesses
* Fulfillment providers
* Manufacturers
* Specialty producers

---

## Challenges

Many production partners operate using manual workflows, emails, spreadsheets, and disconnected systems.

Common challenges may include:

* Incomplete order information
* Artwork approval delays
* Customer communication overhead
* Production status tracking
* Shipping coordination
* Capacity management

These challenges create friction for both creators and production partners.

---

## Foundry's Role

Foundry aims to create a shared operational environment where creators and production partners can collaborate more effectively.

The goal is not to replace existing production systems.

The goal is to improve visibility, communication, workflow management, and operational efficiency between both sides.
